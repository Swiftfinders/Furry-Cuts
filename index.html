Meet Your Groomer

Master Groomer & Founder

With over 12 years of professional grooming experience and a lifelong passion for animal care, I founded Furry Cuts to create the grooming experience I always wished existed—one where pets feel safe, comfortable, and genuinely cared for.

I hold certifications from the Canadian Professional Pet Stylists Association and have trained in specialized techniques for anxious and senior pets. But beyond the credentials, what drives me is the joy of seeing a nervous pup transform into a confident, happy companion who actually looks forward to grooming day.

Every pet has a unique personality, and I take pride in learning what makes each one comfortable. Whether it's extra cuddle breaks, a favorite treat, or just a gentle voice, I ensure your furry family member receives the individualized care they deserve.

"We treat every pet like family—because to you, they are."
